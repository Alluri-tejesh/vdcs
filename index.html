Vardhaman’s Center For Data Science

The Center for Data Science (CDS) is the focal point for Vardhaman efforts in Data Science. The Center was established with goal of creating a leading Data Science training and research facility, and arming researchers and professionals with the tools to harness the power of Big Data with cross-disciplinary research and innovative educational programs, CDS is shaping the new field of Data Science.

Vision

The vision is to expand the data science research, industry collaborations, and the availability of cutting-edge technologies to Empower Citizens for smarter Societies.

Mission

The mission of the Center is to facilitate the quality data science education,research and industrial collaboration that has significant benefits for the individual, industry and
society by enabling better decision making
